Invoice System

Invoices are generated for units during the construction lifecycle.

Typical reasons for invoices include:

milestone payments

optional upgrades

variation costs

extra construction work

Invoice states
Status	Meaning
Draft	Being prepared
Pending Approval	Waiting for review
Approved	Ready to send
Sent	Delivered
Paid	Payment received
Invoice ownership

Invoices belong to units.

Blocks do not contain invoices.

This rule ensures financial traceability.

Exception (rare)

Some accounting exports may group invoices per block for reporting purposes.

This does not change the ownership of invoices.

Invoices remain linked to units internally.

Invoicing completion

A unit can be marked as:

INVOICING_COMPLETED

This indicates that all expected invoices have been generated.

However, additional costs may still occur later.

When this happens, a separate invoice must be created.
